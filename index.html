About Swaramanjusha

Swaramanjusha is a dedicated archive for Indian Classical Music, created to preserve and share the timeless compositions of the legendary maestros.

Our mission is to provide students, connoisseurs, and music lovers with easy access to rare and precious recordings, ensuring that our rich musical heritage continues to inspire generations to come.
